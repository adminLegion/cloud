## What we do

Legion Cloud helps mid-market and growth-stage companies modernize their cloud stack. We are a **boutique**, not a factory: small senior teams, one client at a time, outcomes over hours.

## Services

- **Cloud Migration** (AWS · GCP · Azure) — assess → plan → migrate → optimize.
- **Cloud Architecture Review** — audit and roadmap.
- **Data Platforms** — design and implementation, batch and streaming.
- **FinOps** — cloud cost optimization.
- **Platform Engineering / DevOps** — IaC, CI/CD, internal platforms.

> Detailed methodology and deliverables live in [`.claude/strategy.md`](./.claude/strategy.md) and public playbooks will be added to `playbooks/` as they are written.

## Why Legion Cloud

- **Senior-only teams** — no junior-heavy staffing ratios.
- **Founder involvement** on marquee engagements.
- **Cross-practice leverage** — unique access to our [Quantum](https://github.com/adminLegion/quantum) and [Defense](https://legioncloud.dev) divisions.
- **Modern tooling from day one** — infrastructure as code, observability, AI-assisted engineering.
